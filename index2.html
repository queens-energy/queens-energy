Introduction

This project investigates energy usage at Queens' College to try to uncover patterns in heating, cooling, and temperature behavior across the buildings. Our goals are to cut energy costs, reduce our carbon footprint, and increase comfort of heating for residents.

Data Sources

  • Belimo sensor data
  • Building management system (BMS) data from Priva
  • Fuel usage data from SystemLink

Progress

We have collected and cleaned data from the Belimo sensors installed in Fisher and Cripps. These gave information about heating power, temperatures, and volume flow. Some of the data from these sensors is shown in the graphs below.

We are also in the process of building user interfaces, which will display key information about live energy usage and heating information at Queens'.

In order to analyse and store BMS data from the Priva system, we developed a Python module to interact with the Priva API. We are in the process of setting up long term storage for this data so that future projects will have access to much more data, which will aid analysis.

Currently, we are looking at important places to add more sensors in the heating systems around Queens'. This will again facilitate more comprehensive future analysis.
