Introduction

This project investigates energy usage at Queens' College to try to uncover patterns in heating, cooling, and temperature behavior across the buildings. Our goals are to cut energy costs, reduce our carbon footprint, and increase comfort of heating for residents.

Data Sources

  • Belimo sensor data
  • Building management system data from PRIVA
  • Fuel usage data

Progress

We have collected and cleaned data from the Belimo sensors, which give information about heating power, temperatures, and volume flow. Some of the data from these sensors is shown in the graphs below.

We have also built a working template for a user interface, which will eventually show live information about the heating system and daily energy usage, alongside alerts about potential maintenance issues.
